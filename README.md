# school-cool
Get ya grades!

Registrar for students by students.

You will be able to login and enter your classes and grades, maintaining control of your school life through responsible scheduling and logging.
Wishlist interesting topics and search for your next term's ideal classes through tags. 
Tired of flipping through different websites to figure out what's happening with your year? One stop shop here!
School is cool and fun when you use School Cool!
